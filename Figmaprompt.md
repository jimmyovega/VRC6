# VRC6 Prototype
## What is VRC6? 
VRC6 is an online publication with a focus on the alternate lifestyle.
## What is the format for VRC6?
Users navigating to vrc6.com will see a front page that is a single page hub. There should be no extra space to scroll down to. Every element of the front page should be contained in one single page, regardless of browser window size.
It has a header and footer no matter what is the size of the browser window. The header has a logo on the left and a Contact Us link to the right. 
## What is the styling for VRC6?
The front page is a bento grid style.
In landscape mode, it has two columns. The left column has two pills. One is a large featured article pill, the bottom is a "Please Donate" pill. The right column has 4 pills. Each pill in each column other than the large featured article pill should be the same heigth and width. The right column has 3 Articles pills and 1 Categories pill
## What is the theme for VRC6?
The theme is a variety of pixelated green:
Deep Moss: #052e16 (Background/Shadows)
Forest: #14532d (Mid-tones)
Lime: #4ade80 (Primary Accents)
Phosphor: #bbf7d0 (Highlighter/Text)
The logo should have a pixel monotype font. The page should invoke a retro cyberpunk feel to it.
